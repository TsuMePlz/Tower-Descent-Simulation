About the Game

Tower Descent Simulation is a strategic roguelike game inspired by My Hero Academia. Take command of U.A. High School's Class 1-A as they navigate through a treacherous underground training facility spanning 20 increasingly difficult zones.

Deploy students strategically, manage resources carefully, and make split-second tactical decisions in turn-based combat. Every character has unique abilities, strengths, and weaknesses. Zone environments affect character performance - fire zones boost Bakugo but weaken Asui, while underwater areas reverse these effects.

The deeper you descend, the more challenging it becomes. Boss fights await at critical junctures, and the final encounter with All For One adapts based on your performance - he grows stronger with every student you lose.

Can you guide all 21 students safely to the bottom? Or will casualties mount as the pressure increases? The choice is yours, hero.

How to Play

Objective: Descend through all 20 zones and defeat All For One at the bottom.

Gameplay Loop:

  • Select a student to deploy into the current zone
  • Navigate through procedurally generated rooms
  • Search points of interest for items, survivors, and secrets
  • Engage in tactical turn-based combat
  • Defeat the zone boss to progress deeper
  • Repeat until you reach the final boss

Key Systems:

  • Global Skills: Permanent upgrades that benefit all characters
  • Personal Skills: Powerful character-specific bonuses (lost if captured)
  • Capture System: Fallen students can be rescued in later zones
  • Passive Healing: Benched characters heal while others fight
  • Team-Ups: Devastating combination attacks at Level 10+
  • Plus Ultra: Emergency full recovery (once per zone)
